About Us

Emily and I both started climbing at the UMD Rock Wall. We first met at a club climbing practice, but didn't really get to know each other until we were both working as staff at the wall the next year. Both of us are passionate climbers and enjoy the outdoors. We've both competed at and won a few local climbing competitions.

Post climb dinners are common and pho places make easy dates and cheap meals. There are several pho restaurants around College Park and we would often rate and compare each place to another. One day we joked that we should travel the world to climb and try pho. This blog is the first step of that dream.
