The Ascent

Wall and Climbs

The wall had about 40-50 climbs, spread across 9 ropes. There was a good range of climbs, ranging from 5.5 to 5.12. There were 3 cracks and good aretes and corners for stemming climbs. One of the corners had very nice features, with a sort of person sized box dented inside the corner.
