Pho Thom: A Review

Quick service, right on Route 1

Posted by Kevin Feng on November 24, 2018

Located at 7313 Baltimore Ave F, College Park, MD 20740

Cost


Pho

Small Bowl: $7.95
Large Bowl: $8.95

Not the most expensive pho out there, but I've had better for less.

Service

Quick. Got our food within 10 minutes. Staff know whats up.

Taste

Broth was standard good.
Meat ratio held up well.
Just enough noodles.

Vibe

Chill vibe, good for date or friend date.

Thai Tea

Never had it in a mug before. Sweet, but not too much so.

Overall

A solid pho place with decnet food, good service, and a generally relaxed vibe. Right on route 1 so it is extremely convienent for any UMD stuent looking for a nice sit down place for lunch or dinner. I'm always left very satisfied and full.

PS: This establishment does NOT have fall-proof spoons (with the hook on the end)
